History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a major international conference, attracting thousands of attendees from around the globe.

In 2015, TechCon expanded its focus to include emerging technologies like AI and blockchain, solidifying its position as a leading voice in the tech industry.

Mission

Our mission is to connect innovators, inspire new ideas, and drive the future of technology. We strive to provide a platform for learning, networking, and collaboration.
